# 🍹 Coke Sales Analysis – Excel Project

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes Coca-Cola sales data across major U.S. retailers using Microsoft Excel. The goal is to understand sales performance, profitability, regional trends, and seasonal patterns to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales across retailers and regions
- Study revenue, units sold, and operating margin
- Identify seasonal trends in sales
- Provide business recommendations

---

## 📊 Dataset Information
The dataset includes:
- Retailer
- Region
- Sales Revenue
- Units Sold
- Price per Unit
- Operating Margin
- Date

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts (Bar, Line)
- Slicers & Filters

---

## 📈 Key Analysis
- Retailer-wise performance comparison
- Region-wise sales distribution
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Profitability (Operating Margin) analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Sales performance varies across retailers and regions
- High sales do not always mean high profit margins
- Clear seasonal patterns in demand
- Certain retailers consistently outperform others

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing regions
- Improve pricing strategy in low-margin areas
- Optimize supply chain during peak seasons
- Replicate strategies of top-performing retailers

---

## 🚀 Outcome
This analysis helps improve decision-making by identifying key drivers of sales and profitability.
